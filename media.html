Media






Tourism, is the next multi-billion rupees industry of Khyber-Pakhtunkhwa, the number of domestic and International tourists visiting Khyber Pakhtunkhwa have massively increased, 63% increase in Tourism was recorded in Galiyat 1 year back, the provision of basic facilities like Camping Pods in Thandiani, Sharan, Bishigram and Kumrat, opening of Government Rest houses for public, eliminating NOC condition for foreigners and better communication has led to an increase in the number of tourists.
